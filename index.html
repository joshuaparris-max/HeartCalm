Sound & feedback

Recommended: 4–6 breathing — inhale 4s through the nose, exhale 6s through the mouth, no holds (~6 breaths/min). The longer exhale is deliberate and calming, and there are no breath-holds. Turn on Sound for a gentle tone that rises as you breathe in and falls as you breathe out. Dim lowers the circle's glow for sensitive eyes.

Reflux precautions

  • Avoid large meals at night
  • Don't lie down straight after eating
  • Finish dinner at least 3 hours before bed
  • Raise your upper body slightly if you get a night cough / reflux
  • Ease off spicy / fatty trigger foods for now

Viral / cough support

  • Rest more than usual
  • Drink water steadily
  • Saline nasal spray if needed
  • Warm shower steam is okay — avoid burns
  • Honey in warm water may soothe a cough
  • Avoid stimulant decongestants unless a pharmacist/doctor says okay

While symptoms are active — do not

  • No intense exercise until your GP has reviewed this
  • No stimulant decongestants unless a pharmacist/doctor says okay
  • No potassium supplements or salt substitutes unless advised
  • Don't stack magnesium or other supplements as a "fix"
  • No carotid (neck) massage or other DIY manoeuvres
  • No doom-searching symptoms while you're in it — breathe first

Questions to ask

  • Can we check FBC, electrolytes (incl. magnesium/calcium), kidney function, thyroid, iron/ferritin?
  • Should Holter / event monitoring be repeated or brought forward?

  • Are there signs cardiology should be brought forward?
