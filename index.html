Sound & feedback

Default: inhale 4s through the nose, exhale 6s through the mouth, no holds (~6 breaths/min). The longer exhale is deliberate and calming. Turn on Sound for a gentle tone that rises as you breathe in and falls as you breathe out.

  4. One hand on your belly, one on your chest

Reflux precautions

  • Avoid large meals at night
  • Don't lie down straight after eating
  • Finish dinner at least 3 hours before bed
  • Raise your upper body slightly if you get a night cough / reflux
  • Ease off spicy / fatty trigger foods for now

Viral / cough support

  • Rest more than usual
  • Drink water steadily
  • Saline nasal spray if needed
  • Warm shower steam is okay — avoid burns
  • Honey in warm water may soothe a cough
  • Avoid stimulant decongestants unless a pharmacist/doctor says okay

GP / cardiology prep

Questions to ask

  • Can we do a 12-lead ECG?
  • Can we check FBC, electrolytes (incl. magnesium/calcium), kidney function, thyroid, iron/ferritin?
  • Should Holter / event monitoring be repeated or brought forward?
  • Could asthma, reflux, viral illness, medication, thyroid, anaemia or electrolytes be contributing?
  • Are there signs cardiology should be brought forward?
